Events, Outings, Ramble & Much More

2023

Convocation Photo Service

As Jadavpur University successfully conducted the 66th Annual Convocation, JUPC also successfully carried out the convocation photo service for the degree and medal recipients. With an evergrowing family, the members of JUPC toiled all day near OAT as well as other parts of the campus to provide a well-coordinated service while making this day a most memorable one, both for the degree recipients as well as themselves.

JUPC Annual Freshers' Quiz '23

The age old tradition of the JUPC Annual Freshers' Quiz was renewed on 29th November 2023 after a long hiatus of three years. A written prelims round was held first, from which 5 teams of two each qualified to the main rounds. Participants then went through 4 gruelling rounds, all pertaining to photography, as the winners emerged victorious.

Winners:
Atrideb Sharma
Irene Banerjee

1st Runners-Up:
Soham Sengupta
Ganesh Gorai

A huge thanks to all the enthusiastic Freshers who took part in the Quiz and made the event a success!

Annual Ramble : Sandakphu

We are delighted to announce the success of our recent adventure, the Tumling - Kalipokhri- Sandakphu - Phalut - Gorkhey - Srikhola Ramble! The journey was filled with breathtaking landscapes, unforgettable moments, and of course, some stunning photographs captured by our members. We'd like to express our gratitude to our guide Mingma Sherpa, and every club member who played a vital role in ensuring the success of the Sandakphu Ramble. Looking back on this remarkable journey, we can take a moment to reflect on the bonds we have formed, the skills we have developed, and the fond memories we have shared. We look forward to more adventures and captivating images in the future.

Basic Training Session '23

Theory classes were based on the basics of camera operations, compositions and post processing. The practical classes were held at Victoria memorial, Gourdaha Village and at the campus itself.

Farewell '23

"Goodbyes aren't forever. Goodbyes aren't the end. They simply mean We'll miss you until we meet again"

All the existing club members thanked the batch passing out in 2023 and said the final note of good-bye from the club with a note of grief.

Annual Ramble : Benaras

Club members visited Benaras-The City of Temples for amazing street photographs, the magical lanes of Benaras, Ganga Arati at Dashashmedh Ghat and much more.
